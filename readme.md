# cv_heat_exposure

## Data Used
 - [PRISM](https://prism.oregonstate.edu/explorer/bulk.php) datasets for Central Valley cities (full list in files folder as .csv file) for daily precipitation, minimum, maximum and mean temperatures, minimum and maximum vapor pressure deficit and mean dewpoint temperatures between 1995 and 2025
 - [gridMET](https://www.climatologylab.org/gridmet.html) datasets for daily surface downwelling solar radiation (SRAD) and wind speed at 10 meters (vs) between 1995 and 2025
 - [EPA Air Quality System](https://aqs.epa.gov/aqsweb/airdata/download_files.html#Daily) for daily PM2.5, Ozone and AQI measurements between 1995 and 2025
 - [California Health and Human Services](https://data.chhs.ca.gov)'s datasets for 'Hospital Emergency Department - Characteristics by Patient County of Residence', 'Languages Spoken by Patients and Hospital-Based Providers in California', 'Hospital Emergency Department - Characteristics by Facility (Pivot Profile)' and 'Hospice Utilization - Patient Demographics'
